Dr. Zhixiang Ren is currently an Associate Research Fellow and the head of the "AI for Science Lab" at Peng Cheng Laboratory, as well as a PhD Supervisor at the Southern University of Science and Technology. He is also a co-PhD advisor at Tsinghua University and is recognized as a high-level talent in Shenzhen. He received his Ph.D. from the University of New Mexico (Albuquerque, USA) in 2018 and has worked at both the University of New Mexico and Sun Yat-sen University. His research interests include deep learning, AI for bioinformatics/biochemistry, multimodal large AI models, AI-generated content (AIGC), intelligent temporal data processing, and high-performance computing. He has published over 50 papers with more than 7,000 citations. He has been granted 6 national invention patents and holds 1 software copyright. He has led the development of 1 international standard (ITU-T F.748.18) and 2 industry standards (T/AI 118.1—2022, T/AI 118.2—2022) in the field of intelligent computing. He has also directed the artificial intelligence performance evaluation for the ultra-large-scale intelligent computing system "Pengcheng Cloud Brain II," securing multiple championships in the "AIPerf500" list. He serves as an associated editor of "Big Data Mining and Analytics," "CAAI Artificial Intelligence Research," and "Frontiers in Big Data," and is an experienced reviewer for multiple renowned journals, including "Neural Networks," "Pattern Recognition," "Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence," and "Machine Learning Science and Technology."
